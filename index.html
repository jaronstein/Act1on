Our Mission

Voting is just the start of the political process. A lot of policy and decision-making happens on the federal, state, and local level day to day, which can feel like a fairly closed process. ACT1ON helps you take one quick step each day to get educated, stay informed, and engage in the important decisions your elected officials make everyday. Ready to get started?

How It Works

We just need a little bit of information to get started. Enter your email and select a few issues that you are especially passionate about, so that we can be sure your daily actions are topics you care about. Next, we’ll ask for your zip code so we can send you actions for your area and representatives. Your information is secure, and we’ll never sell your e-mail or other information. We’ll send you just one action e-mail a day, and you can opt out at any time. Your actions will be tailored to your interests, and will include recommended events, phone calls, webcasts, and opportunities to learn more about the issues you care about. Your actions are easy, effective ways to get informed a nd involved. Let’s do this!

Who We Are

We are citizens just like you, looking to get involved and ensure government represents us. We’re based out of Austin, Texas, where we work in the tech field. Technology poses amazing opportunities to share political information quickly and easily, but sometimes gives way to “slacktivism”--and signing an e-petition isn’t enough. We’re here to help!

We believe in participatory democracy. Our government should work for us. To ensure we are represented, all of us need to make political action a habit, every day. With small actions, together we can change politics and make a big difference. Want to know more? Follow us on Facebook and Twitter, and let’s chat. (Buttons for FB and Twitter)
